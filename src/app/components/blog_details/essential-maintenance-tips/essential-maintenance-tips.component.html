5 Essential Maintenance Tips for Your Sugarcane Machine

Technical Team Lion Machine Industries

Regular maintenance is crucial for ensuring the longevity and optimal performance of your sugarcane juice machine. Here are five essential maintenance practices that every business owner should follow.

1. Daily Cleaning Routine

A thorough daily cleaning routine is essential for maintaining hygiene and preventing buildup:

  • Clean rollers after each use with food-grade sanitizer
  • Flush the juice collection tray with clean water
  • Wipe down external surfaces with sanitizing solution
  • Remove and clean detachable parts thoroughly

Pro Tip: Use warm water (not hot) for cleaning to prevent damage to machine components.

2. Regular Roller Maintenance

The extraction rollers are the heart of your machine:

  • Check roller alignment weekly
  • Inspect for wear and tear monthly
  • Lubricate moving parts as recommended
  • Adjust roller pressure when needed

Maintenance Schedule: Replace rollers every 12-18 months depending on usage.

3. Motor and Belt Care

Proper motor maintenance ensures consistent performance:

  • Check belt tension monthly
  • Listen for unusual noises
  • Keep motor housing clean and ventilated
  • Monitor operating temperature

Warning: Never operate the machine with loose or damaged belts.

4. Safety System Checks

Regular safety checks prevent accidents and ensure compliance:

  • Test emergency stop buttons weekly
  • Verify safety guard functionality
  • Check electrical connections monthly
  • Maintain proper grounding

5. Preventive Maintenance Schedule

Follow a structured maintenance schedule:

  • Keep detailed maintenance logs
  • Schedule professional inspections
  • Stock essential spare parts
  • Update maintenance procedures
